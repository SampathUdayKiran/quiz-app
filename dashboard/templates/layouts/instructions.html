Exam Instructions

General Instructions

  1. This is a closed-book, closed-notes exam. You may only use the materials provided by the instructor.
  2. You will have ten minutes to complete the exam.
  3. Read the instructions for each question carefully before answering.
  4. Do not leave any questions unanswered.
  5. You are not allowed to communicate with any other student during the exam.
  6. Navigation between the Questions is not allowed

Technical Instructions

  1. Make sure your device is fully charged before the exam.
  2. You must have a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  3. You must use the provided browser for the exam. Do not open any other tabs or windows.
  4. If you experience any technical issues write a mail to our support team.

Violation of any of these instructions may result in a penalty, including a deduction of points or a failing grade for the exam.
